Ki Jingthmu jong ka Seng Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh

Kat kum ka Riti Treikam (Constitution) 1999 jong ka Seng Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh, ki jingthmu jong ka Seng Kur ki long kumne harum:

  • Ban ia tip ia ithuh, ban wan rah ia ka jingsngew-jan sngew-ieit hapdeng jongngi shi kur, bad ban iarap ia ki ha ka khia ka shon.
  • Ban ia kyrshan bad ia kyntiew iwei ia iwei ha ka longbriew manbriew bad ha ka imlang sahlang.
  • Ban pynshlur bad hikai ia ki parakur ia ki jingmlien ban kynshew kyndiap.
  • Ban lum ban lang man ka por ia ka khanasamari (census) jong ka kur ka jaid.

Na ka bynta kitei ki jingthmu, ka Seng Kur kan:

  1. pynshlur ia ki para kur ban ia snoh kti lang ban iarap ha ka shitom ka lanot, ka iap ka im.
  2. pynshlur ia ki para kur ha ka pule puthi bad ka thoh ka tar da kaba ai khusnam bapher bapher.
  3. pynphriang ia ki jingnang jingtip, ban rah bad kyntiew ia ka jingkoit jingkhiah, ka bam ka dih, ka rep ka riang, ka longbriew manbriew bad ha ka imlang sahlang.
  4. pynshlur ban ym tyngkai ia la ka por, ka dor bad ka bor ha kaba iarap ia ki para kur ha ki kam ki jam bapher bapher, kynthup ruh ia ki lad ioh jingmyntoi na ka sorkar.
  5. kyrsiew bad pynshlur ia ki para kur ban pynnehg pynsah ia ka akor ka burom, ka shongkha shongman da kaba pynlong ki jingialang sneng, jingiaphylliew jingmut bad kiwei kiwei.
  6. pynshlur ia ki parakur ban pynroi-pyniar ia ki sap ki phong bapher bapher.
  7. pynshlur ha ka kynshew kyndiap bad wanrah ia ka mynsiem iatrei lang bad iarpa iwei ia iwei hapdeng shikur da kaba pyntreikam ia “Ka Pla Pisa Ban ia Myntoi Lang”.

Ka Jingseng ia ka Seng Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh

Ki khana pateng ki iathuh ia ngi ba ha ka snem 1711, ha ka sngi ba ki Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh ki la ia poi lang ha Sohrarim ban pynlong ia ka jingiathep Mawbah, ban buh ia ki shyieng jongki ba la khlad noh, ki la pynlong ruh ia ka Dorbar Kur. Kaei kaba ki ia kren ha kata ka dorbar bad kaei ka rai kaba mih nangta ym ioh jingtip satia ne lap ia ki jingthoh ia ki.

Ka jingpyrshang ban lum lang biang ia ki kur Dkhar Sawkpoh ka la long ha ka snem 1994 ha Sohra hapoh ka jingialam I Ma Jerim J. Sawian, bam kwai ha dwar U Blei, bad I Bah Tainington Dkhar Ngapkynta. Wat la kata ka jingpyrshang kam shym la ialam shaduh ban da seng ia ka seng kur hynrei ka la pynrhem ia ka mynsiem ki para kur dkhar sawkpoh ban ia lum biang, ban ia thir ia thai ia la ka thymmei bad ruh ban seng ia ka Seng Kur kaban kdup ia baroh ki kur dkhar sawkpoh.

Ka jing-ialum lang kaba ar ia ki kur dkhar sawkpoh ka la long ha ka 26 tarik u Nailur, 1998 ha Sohra hapoh ka jingialam i Ma Hamlet B. Ngapkynta, bam kwai ha dwar U Blei. Dei ha kata ka jingialang ba ki kur dkhar sawkpoh ki la ia mynjur lang ban seng noh ia ka Seng Kur jong ka jaid Dkhar Sawkpoh, bad la thung ruh ia ki nongkitkam ba shipor (Adhoc) na ka bynta ka Seng Kmie bad ia ki nongkitkam jong ka Seng Samla Sohra. Hadien kata, la pynlong ruh ia ka Dorbar Pyllun jong ka kur dkhar sawkpoh kaba nyngkong eh ha ka 23 tarik u Kyllalyngkot, 1999 ha Sohra, ha kaba la pynskhem ia ka jingseng ia ka Seng Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh bad ba la thung ia ki nongkitkam kiba pura. Nalor kata, ka Dorbar Pyllun ka la pdiang bad pynskhem ruh ia ka Riti Treikam jong ka Seng Kur. Hadien kata, la register ia ka SengKur katkum ki ain jong ka sorkar ha ka 10 tarik u Kyllalyngkot, 2000.

Ha kaba nyngkong, ka jaka treikam jong ka Seng Kur ka long ha Sohra naduh ba sdang ia ka ha ka 26 tarik u Nailur 1998. Hadien ba la dep register ia ka Seng pat la pynkynriah noh ia ka sha Shillong naduh ka 10 tarik u Kyllalyngkot 2000.

Ka Thymmei jong ka Jaid Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh

Ha ka jingthoh “Wad Ia Ka Thymmei” ba la thoh da I Fr. Francis Kharwanlang ha ka 23-01-1999, I pynpaw ba “haduh mynta (1999) ngam don kawei ka thymmei kaba thikna shaphang ka jingsdang ka kur ka jaid jongngi (Dkhar Sawkpoh)”. I iathuh ba ka ha ka snem 1711 ha ka sngi ba ki Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh ki la ia poi lang (ha Sohrarim) ban pynlong ia ka jingiathep Mawbah (ban buh ia ki shyieng jongki ba la khlad noh), ki la pynlong ruh ia ka Dorbar Kur. Hynrei, ym dulir ne ki jingbuh jingthoh ia kaei kaba ki iakren ha kata ka dorbar.

I Ma H. Arthur Dkhar Sawian ha ka jingthoh jongi halor ka phang “Ka Kur Dkhar” ia kaba la lumthup bad pynmih da ka Seng Kur Dkhar Sawkpoh ha ka snem 2011, hadien haba I la bishar ia ki “thymmei” kiba bun lyngba ki khana pateng ki longshuwa ia ka jaid Dkhar Sawkpoh, I pynpaw ba u Thawlang jong ka Jait Dkhar Sawkpoh u dei u Shan Maram Marbaniang. Kita ki khana pateng** jongki longshuwa jongngi, wat la ki iapher katto katne kawei na kawei ha kaba iadei bad ka history ka jaid Dkhar Sawkpoh, hynrei ki ia kubur pat ba u Thawlang bad ka Iawbei jong ka jaid Dkhar Sawkpoh ki dei u u dei u Shan Maram Marbaniang bad ka Kon ka para u Barman (ne Bamon).

Katkum kita ki khanatang, ki Syiem Sutnga ki ju wallam ia ki dkhar Leh-niam (Brahmin) ban leh ia ka niam ka rukom ne puja ha Nartiang La iathuh ruh ba kawei ka kmie Dkhar ka la wan poi lang ha Nartiang ryngkat bad kita ki dkhar leh niam (Brahmin). Ka don sawngut ki khun, ka Tuli, ka Tula, ka Haji bad ka Haja. Kawei na ki khana pateng ka iathuh iangi ba ynda kita sawngut ki khun ki lah heh lah san, ki la ia poikha bad ki riewlum ne trai shnong.

Ka Haja ka don lai ngut ki khun. Kaba nyngkong ka sah ha Nartiang, bad don na ki khun ki ksiew jong ka kiba la ia saphriang sha Nongbah, sha Tuber bad sha ki thain kiba ia marjan. Uba ar u khun jongka u dei u Barman bad kaba lai pat ka dei ka Kon. Ki khana pateng wat la ki iapher kawei na kawei pat kumno ba kine baroh arngut ki poi wir sha Maram, hynrei ki iathuh ba u Barman (ne Bamon) bad ka Kon ki la poi wir sha Maram (Sakwang) bad ba ka Kon la ia kynduh bad poikha bad u Shan Maram Marbaniang, uba na ka shnong Rangthon kaba hap hapoh ka Hima Maram.

Kita ki khana pateng ki iathuh ia ngi ba ka Kon bad u Shan ki la ioh sawngut ki khun kynthei, Ka Sa, ka Phan, ka Wan bad ka Ngap. Bad na kine, ynda ki la ia poikha, la mih ki Sawian na ka Sa bad u Wian, ki Phanbuh na ka Phan bad u Buh, Ki Wanlang na ka Wan bad u Lang, bad ki NgapKynta na ka Ngap bad u Kynta ne Canto (uba dei u Dkhar na Pandua). La iathuh ruh ba Ka Sa, ka Phan bad ka Wan bad la ki lok bad ki khun ki kti jongki ka la poi shong poi sah noh hadien ha Mawmluh. Katba nang iaid ki por, la ka dei na ka daw jong ka jingtrei ka ktah, ka khaii ka pateng ne ka shongkha shongman, ki khun ki kti jong kita ki Sawkpoh ki la ia krih bad saphriang kylleng ka ri khasi bad jaintia, bad don napdeng jongki kiba la kylla kyrteng noh ia ka jaid, kum ki Kharwan ha Langkyrdem.

Ban lap ban shem ia kaba thikna ia ka thymmei bad ia ka history jong ka jaid Dkhar Sawkpoh, ia ka jingsaphriang jongka ha kylleng ki lum khasi bad jaintia bad ban buh jingthoh ia kita, kane ka long ka thong kaba mangi bad ki longdien jongngi ngin hap ban noh synniang.
